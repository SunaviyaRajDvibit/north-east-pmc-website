Client

A Client is the Starting Point Of Every Project, Providing The Vision, Goals, And Requirements. They Share Ideas, Expectations, and Resources to Bring A Concept To Life. The Client’s Role Is Crucial As They Define The Direction, Approve Milestones, And Ensure The Final Outcome Meets Their Needs.

PMC Head

The PMC Head is the leader responsible for overseeing the entire project management cycle. They ensure smooth coordination between clients, engineers, and teams. With strong decision-making skills, the PMC Head manages resources, monitors progress, and resolves challenges, ensuring that every project is delivered on time, within budget, and with quality.

PMC Engineer

The PMC Engineer plays a vital role in executing project plans with precision and technical expertise. They handle design reviews, site inspections, and quality checks while coordinating with contractors and teams. Their focus is on safety, efficiency, and adherence to standards, ensuring projects progress smoothly from planning to completion.
